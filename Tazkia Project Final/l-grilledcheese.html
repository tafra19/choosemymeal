Spicy Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 slices white bread
  • 2 slices American cheese
  • 1 tomato, thinly sliced
  • Â¼ small onion, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, chopped

Directions:

Step 1: Heat a large skillet over low heat. Spread butter or margarine onto one side of two slices of bread. Place both pieces buttered side down in the skillet.

Step 2: Lay a slice of cheese on each one, and top with slices of tomato, onion and jalapeno. Butter one side of the remaining slices of bread, and place on top buttered side up.

Step 3: When the bottom of the sandwiches are toasted, flip and fry until brown on the other side.
