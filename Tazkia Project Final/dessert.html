Crepes

Ingredients:

  • 1 c. all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp. granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 c. whole milk
  • 1 tbsp. butter
  • Fresh fruit, for serving
  • Powdered sugar, for serving

Directions:

Step 1: In a large mixing bowl whisk the flour, sugar and salt until combined. Then create a well with flour and add the eggs. Gradually pour in milk, whisking to combine after each addition. Let batter stand at room temperature until slightly bubbly on top, 15 to 20 minutes.

Step 2: In a small skillet over medium heat, melt butter. About 1/4 cup at a time, drop batter evenly onto pan, swirling it to evenly coat.

Step 3: Cook 2 minutes, then flip and cook 1 minute more. Repeat with remaining batter.

Step 4: Serve crêpes warm with fresh fruit and powdered sugar.

Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 4 oz chocolate chips

Directions:

Step 1: In a large bowl, whisk together the sugars, salt, and butter until a paste forms with no lumps. Add egg and vanilla and whisk again.

Step 2: Add in flour and baking soda, then fold with a spatula. Fold in the chocolate chips. Scoop the dough onto a parchment paper lined baking sheet.

Step 3: Bake for 12-15 minutes in the oven to 350°F (180°C). Cool before serving.

Fudgy Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 cup sugar see note
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Directions:

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C)

Step 2: In a medium bowl combine melted butter and cocoa and sugar stir until fully dissolved. Add eggs one at a time then vanilla and stir until well combined. Stir in flour and salt until the flour is fully combine.

Step 3: Spread mix in a pan and bake for approximately 20-22 minutes or until the center is slightly set. Be careful not to over-bake!

Step 4: Cool completely then cut into 9 large squares or 16 small squares
