Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • ½ small avocado
  • ½ teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • ⅛ teaspoon Kosher salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 (1 oz.) slice whole grain bread, toasted
  • ½ teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • Toppings: sea salt flakes, red pepper flakes

Directions:

Step 1: In a small bowl, combine avocado, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Gently mash with the back of a fork.

Step 2: Top toasted bread with mashed avocado mixture. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle over desired toppings.
