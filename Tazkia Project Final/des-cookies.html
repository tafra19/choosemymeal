Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 4 oz chocolate chips

Directions:

Step 1: In a large bowl, whisk together the sugars, salt, and butter until a paste forms with no lumps. Add egg and vanilla and whisk again.

Step 2: Add in flour and baking soda, then fold with a spatula. Fold in the chocolate chips. Scoop the dough onto a parchment paper lined baking sheet.

Step 3: Bake for 12-15 minutes in the oven to 350°F (180°C). Cool before serving.
