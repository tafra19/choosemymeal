Fudgy Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 cup sugar see note
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Directions:

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C)

Step 2: In a medium bowl combine melted butter and cocoa and sugar stir until fully dissolved. Add eggs one at a time then vanilla and stir until well combined. Stir in flour and salt until the flour is fully combine.

Step 3: Spread mix in a pan and bake for approximately 20-22 minutes or until the center is slightly set. Be careful not to over-bake!

Step 4: Cool completely then cut into 9 large squares or 16 small squares
