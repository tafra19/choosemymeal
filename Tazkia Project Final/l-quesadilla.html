Chicken Quesadillas

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound cubed chicken breast meat
  • 1 packet fajita seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 green bell peppers, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 10 flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese

Directions:

Step 1: Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Stir in the green bell peppers, onion, and chicken. Cook and stir until the vegetables have softened, about 10 minutes.

Step 2: Layer half of each tortilla with the chicken and vegetable mixture, then sprinkle with cheese. Fold the tortillas in half and Place onto a separate pan.

Step 3: Cook until each side is golden brown. Serve Immediately.
